Ocean Blue at the Beach

Above is the balmy beautiful sky’s hue
Presenting an exquisite cerulean blue
Gems are compared to this colour in sight
As their beauty is like the lights of the night

I smell the salt of the deep ocean
Experiencing a rather forgotten emotion
The fragrance sweetened by the ocean’s bellowing breath
Like the winds gentle whisper amidst the season’s breadth

And the strength of the roar of the waves that sing
I lay floating about to what they bring
But in hazy slumber I am overcome
By the taste of the saltwater that submerges some

Raising myself I fight for air
Reminding myself to be more aware
Thankfully watching the currents sway
Looking at the sun to confirm the time of day

In its calmness the water brings joy to my heart
In its close presence and at a distance apart
And soon comes the crested golden sun
Conveying that the day is done

Alas, I await the night to fall
Hearing the sounds of the owl’s brawl
The ebony sky is decorated by the stars above
And with this day and night I fall in love
